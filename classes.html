Heavyweight

By far the most explosive division in the UFC. These guys are some of the biggest & baddest on the planet.

Heavyweight

The heavyweight division in mixed martial arts (MMA) generally groups fighters between 206–265lb (93.4–120.2kg). It is known for being the blockbuster division due to the devastating power of these atheletes.

Light-Heavyweight

The Light heavyweight division in the UFC limits fighters between 186.1-205lbs (84-93kg). This is explosive like the heavyweight division but with slightly more agility.

Welterweight

The welterweight division in the UFC groups competitors within 156-170 lb (71-77 kg). This house major stars such as reigning champion Tyron Woodley and Englishman Darren Till.

Lightweight

The lightweight division in the UFC lightweight division groups competitors within 146-155 lb (66-70 kg).This houses Worldwide sensations like undefeated phenom Khabib Nurmagomedov and Conor McGregor.

Featherweight

The featherweight division in the UFC featherweight division groups competitors within 136-145 lb (61-66 kg).This division is one of the most exciting at the moment with Champ Max Holloway and Rising Star Brian Ortega.
