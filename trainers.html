Pound 4 Pound Rankings

Below is a highlight of the current top 4 pound for pound best fighters in the UFC right now.

#1

Daniel Cormier

Daniel Cormier (born March 20, 1979) is an American mixed martial artist and former Olympic wrestler. He is champion of the heavyweight division. He is the #1 ranked pound-for-pound fighter in the UFC. Cormier is the second of three fighters in UFC history to hold titles in two weight classes simultaneously.

#2

Jon Jones

Jonathan Jones(born July 19, 1987) is an American professional mixed martial artist. He is the current and two-time undisputed UFC Light Heavyweight Champion. Jones held the interim UFC Light Heavyweight Championship, making him a three-time UFC champion overall. Jones was the youngest champion in UFC history at age 23.

#3

Khabib Nurmagomedov

Khabib Nurmagomedov (born 20 September 1988) is a Russian professional mixed martial artist. Nurmagomedov is a two-time Sambo World Champion and reigning UFC Lightweight Champion. He holds the longest undefeated streak in MMA, with 27 wins, and remains undefeated. He is the first Russian and the first Muslim to win a UFC title.

#4

Max Holloway

Max Holloway (born December 4, 1991) is an American mixed martial artist in the Featherweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) where he is the undisputed UFC Featherweight Champion. By age 19 he was 4-0 making him a big prospect from early on. He is #4 in UFC pound-for-pound rankings.
